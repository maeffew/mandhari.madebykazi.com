International web design and development awards

LUFTHANSA

Attracting 226.000 users by making them expect the unexpected

As Lufthansa planned to expand its business with seven new long-haul destinations, they tasked us with creating a website concept that would present those destinations in a way that’s different and innovative. Instead of focusing on pristine beaches and crystal clear ocean as main triggers, we stimulated users to explore the website and book overseas flights with little-known facts about each of the seven destinations.

VIEW CASE STUDY

INFOBIP

Building a customer-centric, fully modular web experience

As a rapidly growing company that entered a new phase in their business journey, Infobip needed a new website that would better represent who they are and what they offer, and also function as a hub for their lead generation activities. They tasked us with creating a website that would be tailored to their users by stepping away from a product-centric approach and embracing the customer-centric one.

VIEW CASE STUDY

MERCURY PROCESSING SERVICES

Rediscovering a fintech’s brand image through innovation

Mercury is a fintech company that was widely considered to be a bank because of its connection to the Sanpaolo banking group. As they were leaving the group, they got the opportunity to rebrand and show who they really are. Our assignment was to create their new brand identity, as well as a website that would present their innovative solutions to clients and attract potential high-performing employees.
