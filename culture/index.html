We live by the Code of Passionate Determination

We strive to grab new possibilities, not to stutter or trip, but to walk with determination, to walk with passion, and decode the unknown and the known.

Our name Bornfight accentuates the can-do spirit and strength of character. The name is the continuous clue for all the Bornfighters to dream and create amazing ideas and solutions. That is the code Bornfighters live by. The Code of Passionate Determination.


Passionate & Determined

We are passionate about technology, learning, and sharing knowledge in the field of digital. We are hard workers and problem solvers, able to understand both the technical and business challenges when creating digital products and experiences.

Progressive

We are curious by nature and we create progress. As we don’t know where our next project will take us, we always wonder what can be done that has never been done before. And strive to create technologically advanced solutions that help people.

Unknown & Known

Known stands for our dig deeper approach, while unknown means that we’ll walk the path no one has gone before. Our commitment to details drives us to test and iterate in every phase of the project, to be sure of the high quality and usability. We decode the unknown and the known, so we can leave our footprints for others.

Our activities

Startupin24h

An annual event where all our passion and talent joins forces as we take on the challenge of building brand new products in just 24 hours.

Meetups

Knowledge is meant for sharing, so we organize events for our community and try to make a contribution to its development.

Weekly educations

We made education an integral part of our work process. Every week we have internal education workshops like “Happy Monday” or “Show-off lunch”.

Active club

We run, swim, dance, do yoga and lift weights. Our health and body is something we don’t take for granted.

Multidisciplinary team

Our team consists of top level designers, coders and experts with a can-do spirit and a dig deeper approach. We constantly experiment with the most advanced technology in order to find a solution for every business challenge.
