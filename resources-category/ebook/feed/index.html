]]> This ebook is a simple positioning guide that explains how to implement a transparent system of professional development into any company. The system explained within this guide enables managers and employees to define the exact level of seniority they or their team members are currently at, what is expected from them at their current positions and what they should do in order to advance to the next level.

Seniority guide is ideal for:

  • CEOs and managers who want to implement a simple and clear system of professional development for employees
  • Team leads and heads of departments who are in charge of creating advancement plans for their team members
  • Employees who want to see what’s expected from them and what they can expect in terms of career development
